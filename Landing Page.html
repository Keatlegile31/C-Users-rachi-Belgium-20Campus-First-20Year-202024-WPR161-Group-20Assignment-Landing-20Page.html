    • Welcome to Strength in Solidarity, a non-profit organization committed to advocating for the rights of women and children in South Africa. Founded in 2016, our mission is to educate, advocate, and campaign for Gender-Based Violence & Femicide (GBVF), Human Rights, and Gender Equality.

    About Strength In Solidarity

    Through our engagement on social media platforms, we reach up to 15 million people monthly, influencing policymakers and public opinion. Strength in Solidarity provides vital resources to victims and families, including medical and legal assistance, counseling services, and information on how to handle GBVF cases.


    Join us in our journey towards a safer and more equitable society. Together, we can create lasting change and stand in solidarity with survivors of gender-based violence.
